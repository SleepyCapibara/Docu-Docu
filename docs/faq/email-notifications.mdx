# Set Your Email Notifications on SOC Prime

A guide on how to personalise email notifications you get from SOC Prime.

<DocAuthors authors={["Eugene"]} date="February 2, 2023" />

Email notifications can be a great tool to get all important updates about newly released content that is relevant specifically to your organization and with a frequency that matches your workload. In this article we will go through all settings you can create.

You can navigate to Email Notification settings from the top right corner → Click on Account and then switch to the Email Notifications tab.

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/01.png)

## Turning on/off Notifications

First things first, if during registration you have disabled email notifications you can turn them on in this menu section. You can also disable getting notifications from here as well at any point, so you are in total control if you want to get this type of emails from us.

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/02.png)

## Frequency

The first thing you can adjust in Email notifications is the frequency of updates you get. You can select from drop-down options to get notified every 1, 3, 5, 7, or 30 days. The email you would get will have bundled information about new and updated content for the selected period, based on the search settings we will discuss next.

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/03.png)

## Search Profile

Search profile allows you to narrow down the notifications about new content at SOC Prime to the ones that are relevant to the SIEM, XDR you use, and more. After you complete SOC Prime’s Onboarding you will automatically create one search profile. You can also select the blank option to get a notification about all of the released content.

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/04.png)

You can create more search profiles [here](https://tdm.socprime.com/integrate-search-profiles/).

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/05.png)

## Content List

Another option for how you can adjust your email notifications is based on content lists you can create for SOC Primes’ published content. If you are looking to get notified of specific types of detections, or new content which is focused on the specific list of APTs, for example, you can do it with this type of Email Notification setting.

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/06.png)

To create your own content lists click [here](https://tdm.socprime.com/ccm/).

## Lucene Search

You can use Lucene search to fine-tune your email notifications. This option will allow you to learn in time about the rules for detecting specific vulnerabilities, selected malware, or attacks of APT groups.

You can check this guide for more details on how to use [Lucene Search](https://help.socprime.com/en/articles/6265772-lucene-syntax) and create your own queries.

![Product screenshot](/img/docs/faq/email-notifications/07.png)

After adding all the necessary settings, click the 'Save Changes' button.

Enjoy.
